About the Recent Senior Records

It all started with Michael George's Senior Rankings pages, and you can read that background here.

Fast forward nearly a decade from its very beginnings and the senior cubing community is thriving worldwide, with over-40s competing in a wide variety of official events every week. This site filters the top recent senior results to see who is out there raising the bar for everyone else.

A single event result may have multiple rankings if it applies to multiple age groups. For example, official results of a 68 year old competitor will be eligible for the over 40s, over 50s and over 60s rankings. More details about the rankings data can be found here.

Note that we only show the most recent records. If someone improves several times in the time-period then only the latest achievement will be shown.

Many thanks to Michael George, this site could not exist without all his work to compile and maintain the senior rankings data.
